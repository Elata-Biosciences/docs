title: "Governance"
---

Elata governance is contract-based and timelocked.

## How Voting Power Works

- lock ELTA into veELTA
- longer locks increase voting power (up to 2x boost)
- voting uses governor + timelock flow

## Proposal Defaults

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Voting delay | 1 day |
| Voting period | 7 days |
| Proposal threshold | 0.1% of supply |
| Quorum | 4% of supply |

Emergency proposal paths can use stricter thresholds and faster windows depending on deployment config.
