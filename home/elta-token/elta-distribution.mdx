ELTA is the native currency of the Elata Ecosystem. Built as ERC-20 with Votes, Permit, and Burnable extensions for:

- Protocol governance
- Gasless approvals
- Real deflation via burns

- Fixed max supply: `77,000,000`
- Minted once at deployment
- Minted to treasury address at deployment (stay tuned in for updates)

## 2. Supply Distribution

Our governance token, ELTA, will be launched as an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain & will be bridged to Base and other blockchains to power fast, low-cost brain usecases & ensure Elata protocol stability.

The total supply of ELTA tokens at launch is 77 million with a hard cap & no future emissions. The token is designed with strong community ownership and long-term sustainability. 

Full breakdown:

**2.1 Public presale** 

The ELTA presale allocation is 7.5% of our token supply, reserved for early believers who backed the vision from day one. Their early support, indicative of their willingness to take on initial risk, is rewarded with a 50% discount on the launch price, for their pivotal role in setting the foundation for Elata. The goal of presale is to fund core development, talent, and the initial Elata protocol infrastructure & apps.

**2.2 Community sale**

13% of the total ELTA supply will be sold to the Elata community for increased decentralization & to give them a final chance to become a part of the ecosystem before launch. Participants will get a discount on the launch price, rewarding their early support before TGE.

**2.3 Strategic Investors**

7% of the total ELTA supply is allocated to long-term aligned partners who bring networks and adoption to the Elata ecosystem. The allocation is kept below community allocation to prioritize decentralization.

**2.4 Liquidity & Market Making**

15% is allocated for liquidity & market making to ensure deep, stable markets from day one for ELTA and successful app token launches. Liquidity pools will be locked for at least 12 months upon deployment across CEXes & DEXes, to prevent manipulation, build trust, and support smooth trading. Unused allocation may later be redeployed upon governance approval.

**2.5 Core Contributors** 

Core contributors will receive 20% of the supply. These early contributors have played a pivotal role in our journey and have laid the foundation for Elata’s success. A 1 year cliff and 4 year linear vesting is enforced to align contributors with the long-term success of Elata. If a contributor leaves within 5 years, the vesting stops, and the remaining allocation is moved to the treasury.

**2.6 Advisors**

Advisors will receive 3% of the supply for playing a pivotal role in the Elata journey by supporting the strategic direction. A 1 year cliff and 4 year linear vesting is enforced to align advisors with the long-term success of Elata.  If an advisor leaves within 5 years, the vesting stops, and the remaining allocation is moved to the treasury.

**2.7 Core Ecosystem**

We've strategically allocated 29.5% to the Elata ecosystem. This provides operational runway, developer bounties, hardware development, fundraising rounds, airdrops, device credit/subsidies, deployment to lending markets & more and is self-funded via revenue splits from the Elata Protocol. Tokens follow fixed 5 year linear vesting and governance can reallocate but not accelerate emission beyond predefined caps.

**2.8 Airdrop**

To reward positive-sum behavior of users and our community prior to launch, we have allocated 5% of our supply as a community airdrop rewarding testers of our Elata Protocol prior to launch and those whose support has been instrumental in making the project come to life. After all airdrop tokens have unvested, any unclaimed tokens will move to the core treasury after 30 days.
