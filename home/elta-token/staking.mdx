Lock your ELTA tokens into the [veELTA](/home/elta-token/veelta) contract to receive vote-escrowed ELTA (veELTA). Longer lock durations earn a higher boost on voting power and a proportional share of protocol fees.

Additionally, individual apps may opt-in and give extra rewards to veELTA holders.

<Info>
  Staking is non-custodial. Your ELTA remains in the immutable `VeELTA` smart contract — no multisig, admin, or third party can move your tokens.
</Info>

## How It Works

The veELTA staking model follows a vote-escrow design. When you lock ELTA, you receive a non-transferable veELTA balance that determines your share of governance power and fee revenue. Your veELTA balance is a function of the amount locked and the duration of your lock.

### Boost Mechanics

Your veELTA balance is calculated using a **linear boost** between `1×` and `2×` based on lock duration:

```text
veELTA = ELTA_locked × boost(duration)

boost(d) = 1 + (d - MIN_LOCK) / (MAX_LOCK - MIN_LOCK)
```

Where `MIN_LOCK = 7 days` and `MAX_LOCK = 730 days (2 years)`.

| Lock Duration | Boost Multiplier | 10,000 ELTA Locked → veELTA |
| :-----------: | :--------------: | :-------------------------: |
| 7 days        | 1.00×            | 10,000                      |
| 6 months      | ~1.25×           | ~12,500                     |
| 1 year        | ~1.50×           | ~15,000                     |
| 2 years       | 2.00×            | 20,000                      |

<Tip>
  Locking for the maximum duration of **730 days** doubles your effective veELTA balance — giving you twice the voting power and twice the fee share compared to the minimum lock.
</Tip>

## Revenue Share

Protocol fees flow through the **V2 Fee Pipeline** (`FeeCollector → FeeSwapper`) and are split across three destinations:

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card icon="lock" title="70% to App Stakers">
    Distributed to users who stake individual app tokens within each app's ecosystem.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="lock" title="15% to veELTA Holders">
    Your share of protocol-wide revenue, proportional to your veELTA balance relative to total veELTA supply.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="vault" title="15% to Treasury">
    Funds protocol development, grants, and ecosystem growth managed through governance.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Revenue originates from three fee sources:

- **Trading fees (1%)**: Charged on every buy/sell on an app's bonding curve
- **Launch fees (10 ELTA)**: Flat fee per new app launch, routed 100% to treasury
- **Tournament rake (2.5%)**: Cut from app tournament prize pools (if individual apps opt in)

<Note>
  If an app is paused, 100% of its fees route to the treasury until it resumes normal operations.
</Note>

## How to Stake

<Steps>
  <Step title="Acquire ELTA">
    Obtain ELTA tokens through the Elata ecosystem via. app bonding curves, community distributions (e.g. airdrops), secondary markets, or by directly contributing to ecosystem growth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the VeELTA Contract">
    Call `approve()` on the ELTA token contract, authorizing the `veELTA` contract to transfer your chosen amount.

    ```solidity
    ELTA.approve(veELTAAddress, amount);
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lock Your ELTA">
    Call `lock()` on the veELTA contract, specifying the amount and lock duration (between 7 and 730 days).

    ```solidity
    veELTA.lock(amount, durationInDays);
    ```

    Your veELTA balance is minted immediately based on the amount and chosen duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participate in Governance">
    Once locked, your veELTA balance enables you to create and vote on proposals through the Elata Governor contract. Proposals require a threshold of ~77,000 ELTA (0.1% of total supply) in veELTA to submit, and quorum is set at 4% of total supply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim Revenue">
    Protocol fees are distributed to veELTA holders on a rolling basis. Claim your share through the rewards distribution mechanism.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Governance Integration

veELTA is the sole source of voting power in the on-chain governance system:

| Governance Parameter        | Value                                          |
| :-------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| **Proposal threshold**      | 0.1% of total supply (~77,000 ELTA equivalent) |
| **Quorum**                  | 4% of total supply                             |
| **Voting delay**            | 1 day                                          |
| **Standard voting period**  | 7 days                                         |
| **Emergency voting period** | 3 days                                         |
| **Timelock delay**          | 48 hours                                       |

<Warning>
  veELTA is non-transferable. You cannot sell, trade, or delegate your veELTA balance. It represents a personal, time-locked commitment to the protocol.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I unlock early?">
    No. ELTA is locked for the full duration you select at staking time. Choose a lock period that matches your commitment level. The minimum lock is 7 days.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when my lock expires?">
    Once your lock duration ends, you can withdraw your full ELTA balance. Your veELTA balance drops to zero, and you stop earning fee revenue and voting power until you re-lock.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I extend my lock duration?">
    Yes. You can extend your lock to increase your boost multiplier without withdrawing. Extending resets your lock period to the new, longer duration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add more ELTA to an existing lock?">
    Yes. You can top up your locked position with additional ELTA. The new tokens inherit your current lock's remaining duration and boost.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the VeELTA contract upgradeable?">
    No. All Elata protocol contracts, including veELTA, are non-upgradeable and immutable after deployment. This eliminates admin-key risk.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Has the contract been audited?">
    The protocol is currently pending an external security audit before mainnet deployment. The contracts are open-source and available for review on [GitHub](https://github.com/Elata-Biosciences/elata-protocol/tree/vNext).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security Design

The staking system is built with these guarantees:

- **Immutable contracts**: No proxy patterns, no upgrade keys
- **Fixed ELTA supply**: 77,000,000 tokens minted once at deployment, no inflation
- **Role-based access**: Admin functions require multisig approval
- **Time-locked governance**: 48-hour delay before any governance action executes on-chain
- **Open source**: Full contract source available for review
