## What App Tokens Are

Each app can launch its own ERC-20 token through the protocol.

App tokens are designed to:

- bootstrap early distribution through an ELTA bonding curve
- support app-level incentive design
- route launch/trading/module fees through a common protocol pipeline

## Launch Defaults

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Launch cost | `110 ELTA` |
| Total supply | `10,000,000` |
| Allocation | `50%` curve, `25%` vesting, `25%` ecosystem |
| Graduation target | `42,000 ELTA` |

## Fee Behavior

- Launch fee kind routes to treasury.
- App-revenue fee kinds route to contributors + treasury.
- Transfer tax is LP-keyed and configurable within bounds.

## Lifecycle

1. Register app
2. Launch token
3. Curve phase
4. Graduation and LP lock
5. Ongoing app-level operations
