## Governance Structure

Elata governance uses on-chain governor and timelock contracts with veELTA voting power.

## Core Parameters (Current Defaults)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Voting delay | 1 day |
| Voting period | 7 days |
| Proposal threshold | 0.1% of supply |
| Quorum | 4% of supply |
| Timelock delay | 48 hours |

## Notes

- veELTA voting power comes from ELTA lock amount and lock duration.
- Critical protocol parameters are timelock-gated.
