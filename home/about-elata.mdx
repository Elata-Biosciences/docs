## Overview

Elata focuses on one clear protocol goal: make app launches and fee routing predictable, transparent, and auditable on-chain.

The protocol provides:

- deterministic launch defaults
- explicit app ownership and contributor splits
- constant-product curve distribution
- fee-kind based routing to treasury and contributors

## Why Onchain

On-chain primitives make launch rules and value flow verifiable:

- who owns an app
- how supply is allocated
- when graduation happens
- where fee proceeds route

## Vision

Build an open ecosystem where builders can ship apps without rebuilding token launch and fee infrastructure from scratch.
