## Immediate Actions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate the bonding curve">
    After Phase B deployment, the curve starts in `PENDING` state. Activate it to begin trading. Early access gating (100 XP required) applies for the first 6 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your app listing">
    Check the app store at [app.elata.bio](https://app.elata.bio) to confirm metadata, logo, and description appear correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up contributor splits">
    Configure the ContributorSplit contract through your Safe. Add team members and set their share percentages. This determines how app revenue is distributed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure social links and team display">
    Add Twitter, Discord, GitHub, and team member info to your app listing so users can find your community.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Launch Week

<Tip>
  The first week sets the tone for your app's community. Focus on visibility and first purchases.
</Tip>

- **Announce** on Twitter and Discord with your app store link
- **Distribute XP** to early supporters so they can participate during the early access window
- **Run an inaugural tournament** to drive engagement and token activity
- **Create an access pass item** if your app has gated features
- **Monitor holder distribution** to ensure healthy early spread

---

## Ongoing Operations

After the initial launch, shift focus to sustained growth:

- **Track graduation progress** toward the 42,000 ELTA threshold
- **Run regular tournaments** to maintain engagement
- **Adjust contributor splits** as your team grows
- **Review analytics** for holder concentration and trading volume
