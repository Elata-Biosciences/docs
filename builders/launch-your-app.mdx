## Overview

Current default launch economics:

- Total cost: `110 ELTA`
- Creation fee: `10 ELTA`
- Curve seed: `100 ELTA`
- Token supply: `10,000,000`
- Allocation: `50/25/25` (curve/vesting/ecosystem)

---

## Launch Paths

### Two-Phase Path (Recommended)

1. `createAppWithoutToken(ownerSafe, metadataURI, contributors)`
2. `launchTokenForApp(appId, name, symbol, supply, operators)`

### Legacy Path

`createApp(...)` performs both phases in one call for compatibility.

---

## What Gets Deployed

- `AppToken`
- `AppBondingCurve`
- `AppStakingVault`
- `AppVestingWallet`
- `AppEcosystemVault`
- `ContributorSplit` (from registration phase)

---

## Post-Launch Flow

1. Curve starts in `PENDING`.
2. After activation delay, curve becomes `ACTIVE`.
3. Buyers purchase on curve (`x*y=k`) until target/deadline.
4. At graduation, LP is created and locked.

---

## Important Notes

- Creator allocation is vesting + ecosystem in current defaults (not auto-staked creator half).
- Transfer tax is LP-keyed and fee-collector routed.
- Launch fees and app-revenue fees route differently by fee kind.
