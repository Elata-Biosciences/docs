## Important Context

Fee routing in current docs is centered on:

- `FeeCollector` accounting
- `FeeSwapper` routing by fee kind
- `ContributorSplit` payouts for app-revenue fee kinds

Because deployments can differ, check the specific app's configured reward paths before staking for yield assumptions.

## What You Can Do

- hold app tokens
- participate in app-level staking where enabled
- monitor app-specific fee and reward configuration
- lock ELTA as veELTA for governance participation

## Before You Stake

1. Verify which contract actually receives that app's routed fees.
2. Verify whether rewards are treasury-only or contributor + treasury for that app state.
3. Verify whether staking contract for that app has active funded reward flow.
